I’m an AI evaluations researcher with the SAgE research group at Princeton University and an AI policy researcher at the Brookings Institution. I research AI's societal impact and aim to inform policymaking.

Currently, I'm working on a few projects related to agent evaluation, AI transparency reporting, and tech diffusion.

Prior to this, I completed my bachelors from Tufts University in Applied Mathematics and STS, where I was advised by Kasso Okoudjou (math) and Nick Seaver (anthropology).
